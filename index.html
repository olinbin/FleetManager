Product Overview

Fleet Manager is a lightweight SaaS management system for international individual users and micro fleets with fewer than 10 vehicles. The product focuses on core functions such as vehicle asset management, trip tracking, expense recording, and maintenance reminders, helping users efficiently manage their vehicles through a simple and intuitive interface.
