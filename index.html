Simple Fleet Management Solution

Fleet Manager is a specialized fleet management software designed for small businesses and micro fleets with fewer than 10 vehicles. Our lightweight fleet management system focuses on essential functions including vehicle asset management, trip tracking, expense recording, and maintenance scheduling, helping you efficiently manage your fleet through an intuitive interface. Our SaaS fleet management solution provides vehicle tracking, expense recording, and trip management tools.

Key Features of Our Fleet Management Software

Our vehicle management system includes comprehensive tools for tracking trips, recording expenses, and scheduling maintenance. The trip tracking system helps you monitor routes and optimize fleet operations, while our expense recording software allows you to keep detailed records of all vehicle-related costs.
