Simple Fleet Management Solution

Fleet Manager is a specialized fleet management software designed for small businesses and micro fleets with fewer than 10 vehicles. Our solution focuses on essential functions including vehicle asset management, trip tracking, expense recording, and maintenance scheduling, helping you efficiently manage your fleet through an intuitive interface.

Why Choose Our Fleet Management Software?

  • Designed for Small Business: Perfectly sized for micro fleets under 10 vehicles
  • No Learning Curve: Get started immediately without extensive training
  • Cost-Effective Solution: Free fleet management software with all essential features
  • All-in-One Platform: Manage vehicles, trips, expenses, and maintenance in one place
  • Accessible Anywhere: Web-based fleet tracking software works on any device
