Product Features

Powerful yet simple tools to manage your fleet efficiently

Vehicle Asset Management

Centralized management of all vehicle information, including basic details, ownership, insurance, and registration status. Easily view and update vehicle data whenever needed.

Trip Tracking

Record and track every trip with details like distance, duration, start/end locations, and purpose. Generate trip reports for better cost analysis and planning.

Expense Recording

Track all vehicle-related expenses, including fuel, maintenance, repairs, insurance, and tolls. Categorize expenses for clear cost analysis and budgeting.

Maintenance Reminders

Set up automatic reminders for routine maintenance tasks like oil changes, tire rotations, and inspections based on mileage or time. Never miss an important maintenance deadline.

Multi-Platform Access

Access your fleet management data from any device with a web browser, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and smartphones. Enjoy a consistent user experience across all platforms.

Data Analytics

Visualize your fleet data with intuitive charts and reports. Gain insights into vehicle usage, costs, and performance to make informed decisions.

User-Friendly Interface

Clean, intuitive design that makes fleet management easy for users of all technical levels. Get started in minutes with no extensive training required.

Secure Data Storage

All your fleet data is securely stored with encryption, ensuring the privacy and safety of your sensitive information. Regular backups protect against data loss.
