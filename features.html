Fleet Management Software Features

Essential tools to efficiently manage your small business fleet

Our fleet management software combines essential features with an intuitive interface, making it the perfect solution for small businesses and micro fleets. As a lightweight fleet management system, each feature is designed to save you time and reduce fleet operating costs. Our SaaS fleet management solution provides vehicle tracking, expense recording, and trip management tools.

Vehicle Asset Management

Centralized management of all vehicle information, including basic details, ownership, insurance, and registration status. Easily view and update vehicle data whenever needed. Our vehicle management system provides comprehensive tracking of your fleet assets.

Trip Tracking

Record and monitor all vehicle trips with start/end locations, distance traveled, and purpose. Generate reports to analyze vehicle utilization and optimize routes. Our trip tracking system helps you monitor fleet efficiency and reduce unnecessary travel costs.

Expense Recording

Track all fleet-related expenses including fuel, maintenance, insurance, and repairs. Categorize costs for detailed financial analysis and budget planning. Our expense recording software provides detailed insights into your fleet's financial performance.

Maintenance Scheduling

Set up maintenance reminders based on time or mileage. Keep track of service history and upcoming appointments to ensure vehicle reliability and longevity. Our fleet tracking system includes intelligent maintenance alerts.

All Features Included in Our Free Plan

Unlike other fleet management solutions, our software includes all features in a single free plan. No hidden fees or premium upgrades required. Our micro fleet software solution provides all the functionality you need without the complexity of enterprise systems.
