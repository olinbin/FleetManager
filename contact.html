Frequently Asked Questions

How much does the fleet management software cost?

Our fleet management software is completely free to use. We believe small businesses deserve powerful fleet management tools without the high price tag.

Is there a free trial available?

Since our fleet management software is entirely free, you automatically start with the full experience. No trial period needed - all features are available from day one.

Do you offer premium features or paid plans?

No, we don't. We provide all fleet management features in our single free plan. Our mission is to make fleet management accessible to small businesses and micro fleets.

How many vehicles can I manage with your software?

Our fleet management software is designed for micro fleets with up to 10 vehicles. Many of our users successfully manage 1-5 vehicles, while some manage the full capacity.

What kind of support do you provide?

We offer email support for all users. While we're a small team, we strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days.
